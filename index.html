About

Bridging academia & the profession.

Founded in 1919 and recognized by the AICPA — the world's largest accounting body — Beta Alpha Psi is the premier honors organization for accounting, finance, and information systems students.

Beta Alpha Psi is an international honors organization for financial information students and professionals. It recognizes academic excellence and bridges the classroom and the career — connecting students directly with the firms, mentors, and opportunities that shape their futures.

The Nu Sigma Chapter at Fairleigh Dickinson University has been an active, award-winning chapter since 2014. We host professional development events, firm site visits, networking panels, and community service — all designed to make our members recruiter-ready on day one.

Membership in BAP is a recognized credential. Our members have gone on to careers at the Big 4, top regional firms, and major financial institutions across the country.

What we do

A year inside Nu Sigma.

Every semester runs on a tight calendar of professional, academic, and service programming — designed to put members in the room with the firms and faculty who shape their careers.

Firm Engagement
Firm visits & recruiter panels

Big 4, regional firms, and Fortune-500 finance teams come to campus — or host us at their offices — for case sessions, recruiting talks, and one-on-ones with members.

Academic Edge
CPA prep & study sessions

Member-led study groups for accounting and finance coursework, plus structured CPA-exam prep led by Becker partners and faculty.

Networking
Alumni & mentor nights

Chapter alumni now at firms across NYC and beyond come back every semester to mentor undergrads through interviews, internships, and offers.

Service
VITA & community outreach

Members serve the surrounding community through VITA tax-prep clinics, financial-literacy workshops in local schools, and chapter-led volunteer drives.

Competition
Best Practices & Nationals

Each year the chapter builds an original project and competes against universities nationwide. In Spring 2026 we won the Innovation Award in Cincinnati — and advanced to the National Championship in Baltimore.

Always-On
Two semesters, one chapter

Fall and spring run on a published cadence of weekly meetings, professional events, and induction milestones — every member knows what's next.

Membership

Join Beta Alpha Psi.

Open to FDU students serious about accounting, finance, or information systems. No dues, no hazing, no fluff — just academic standing and the willingness to show up.

Eligibility Requirements
  • Enrolled at Fairleigh Dickinson University
  • Sophomore standing or above (30+ credit hours)
  • Minimum cumulative GPA — 3.2 undergraduate · 3.0 graduate
  • Majoring in Accounting, Finance, or Information Systems
  • Completed or enrolled in an introductory accounting course
Membership Requirements
  • Attend required chapter meetings and events
  • Participate in the required professional activities
  • Maintain GPA requirements throughout membership
  • Complete chapter service requirements
  • Demonstrate scholarship, professionalism, and service
No membership fees — completely free
